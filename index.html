How to Play

Welcome to ReGenesis, a post-apocalyptic incremental game where you must rebuild civilization from scratch. Follow these steps to thrive in this new world:

  1. Collect Resources: Click the "Collect Resources" button to gather resources. You'll receive between 1 and 3 resources each time.
  2. Build Shelter: Use your resources to build shelters. Each shelter increases your population by 2.
  3. Construct Farms: Build farms to produce food for your growing population. Each farm costs resources and generates food every 3 seconds.
  4. Expand: Continue to build shelters and farms to increase your population and food production.

Functionality

Collect Resources

This function simulates gathering resources from the environment. Each time you click the "Collect Resources" button, you receive between 1 and 3 resources randomly.

Build Shelter

This function allows you to increase your population by building shelters. Each shelter costs resources, and the cost increases by 10% with each purchase. You can see the current cost of building a shelter next to the button.

Build Farm

With this function, you can construct farms to produce food for your population. Each farm costs resources, and the cost increases by 20% with each purchase. Farms generate 5 food every 3 seconds, with each additional farm increasing food production exponentially.

AutoSave and Load Progress

Every 30 seconds the game saves itself. Delete cookies and you lose progress.Also loads itself when you start the game.
